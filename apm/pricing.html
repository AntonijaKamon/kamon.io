Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to pay for instrumentation?

Nope. All Kamon instrumentation has been, is, and will always be free and open source. The instrumentation is released under the Apache V2 license and available on Github.

So, what am I paying for?

Kamon APM is, obviously, an Application Performance Monitoring tool for monitoring and debugging distributed systems. You don't have to pay for anything to try it out, you can start monitoring up to 5 microservices for free right now!

If your needs grow out of our free plan you can upgrade to the GROWTH plan. The cost is super simple and can be boiled down to 1 EUR/service per day or 30 EUR/service monthly. We never start charging without your confirmation.

How do you charge?

The total we are billing each customer at the end of the month depends on the number of active services and the number of additional spans sent. Please see here a detailed breakdown on our pricing model and how an invoiced fee would be calculated.

Do I need a credit card to sign up for free plan?

No credit card required. We want you to pay for Kamon because you have experienced and enjoyed Kamon APM and understood its value.

What if I need a custom plan?

We can accommodate more metrics, spans and retention for you. Contact us through Intercom (the message bubble on the bottom-right corner of the screen) or via email at sales@kamon.io.

What languages/frameworks does Kamon support?

Kamon works best with any JVM language like Java and Scala. But it’s going to work especially awesome with Akka and Play frameworks. For now.

We do plan on adding other frameworks and languages. If there is a language or a framework you would particularly like to see supported, just drop us an email at sales@kamon.io. Just write what you want, don't overthink it.

What is the overhead of monitoring services with Kamon?

Typical performance impact of Kamon is between 1 and 3 percent. If you notice anything above that, please contact our support and we will make sure to optimize it.

How do test environments work?

The non-production environments have limited retention of 24 hours and can be used for monitoring staging, development and pre-production environments, absolutely free of charge.

How often will you charge me?

We will charge your credit card at the beginning of every month for all usage incurred in the previous month.

What type of support is available?

If something went wrong during your setup, you have some feature request or in general just want to hang out with us and talk about your experience with Kamon in general, head over to our Github Discussions and say it all.

Kamon APM users can reach us on Intercom anytime for one-on-one support.

How do I start using Kamon?

It really shouldn't take you more than 3 minutes! Check our Get Started with Kamon page for step-by-step instructions on how to start instrumenting your service. Or go to Kamon APM Onboarding and just follow the instructions there.

What are services and instances, traces and metrics?
